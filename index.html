Cuisine

There are many different cuisines, all tasty, that come from Asia. Some examples of these cuisines are Butter Chicken, Dumplings, Rice, Pho, Adobo, etc. Today, millions of people eat these foods, and there are thousands of restaurants in America that serve these foods.
